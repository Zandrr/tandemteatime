Why create an influencer network?

Finding the right path in life is never straightforward. Making the best decisions is tough — but hearing the perspectives of others can make this process easier.

It's nice to be able to chat with an expert: whether you need advice on a life changing event, or simply want to learn more about a subject that's meaningful to you.

People that have been through similar experiences can provide invaluable advice — but they're often hard to come by. We believe it's important to create a network of meaningful in person relationships. Build connections. Be Tandem.
